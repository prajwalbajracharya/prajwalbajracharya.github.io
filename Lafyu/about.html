Values We Live By

Bold & simple. premier products

Creating simplistic products that add a modern flair to the comfortable & stylish norm. Quality materials strictly justify the standards set for Lafyu’s signature products that are exclusively made in Nepal. Push timeless designs for our customers with a trendy and contemporary look and feel.

Be diverse & inclusive

Striving to build a healthy environment, unchained from any gender, race, or culture indifferences. With open arms to various cultures to work together in harmony. Products that cater to different customer bases with a cohesive and holistic approach to inclusivity throughout their existence is an integral part of Lafyu.

Trust & & collaboration

Be ready to set onto the untrodden roads with an open mindset, working to achieve personal goals. Teamwork makes the dream work - we believe our passion shines best when working in a team with proper guidance & structure. Share your thoughts & ideas - encourage others to move forward.

Ever-changing, open-minded

Like how the water shapes itself into the vessel that contains it, change remains integral to our company's flow. Be ready to embrace diversity with open arms and accept feedback/criticism with positivity. An environment where new ideas thrive - set sail with fresh approaches that help the company stay relevant.

Happy customer, good relations

Face the good & ugly sides of the customers. Build a healthy relationship with customers - present Lafyu as a reliable and trustworthy brand. Like stars in the sky, we want our customers shining with confidence & positivity in the vast, never-ending universe. Treat customers like real people rather than robots.

We are never ending (TM)

We do our best to create high quality clothing wears.

We wanted a simple, relatable & influencing tagline that would be the voice of Lafyu. So we came up with ‘Never Ending'™ as our tagline.Our tagline is cemented into our brand values to create products that our customers appreciate and do not feel outdated.

Therefore, we remember the tagline and say to ourselves, ‘We are Never Ending™.’ This pushes us to always produce new & exciting products for our customers.
